# Acme Corp Employee Handbook

Last updated: January 15, 2025

## 1. Welcome to Acme Corp

Welcome to Acme Corp! Founded in 2018, we are a B2B SaaS company specializing in data analytics solutions. With over 350 employees across offices in San Francisco (HQ), Austin, and London, we are committed to building a workplace where innovation and collaboration thrive.

## 2. Paid Time Off (PTO)

All full-time employees receive 20 days of PTO per year, accrued at 1.67 days per month. PTO does not roll over beyond 5 days into the next calendar year. Employees must submit PTO requests at least 5 business days in advance through the HR portal (hr.acmecorp.internal). Manager approval is required for requests exceeding 5 consecutive days.

Holidays: Acme Corp observes 10 company-wide holidays per year, including New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving (2 days), and Winter Break (December 24-26, December 31).

Sick leave: Employees receive 10 sick days per year. Sick leave of 3 or more consecutive days requires a doctor's note.

## 3. Remote Work Policy

Acme Corp operates a 3/2 hybrid model: employees are expected in-office Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday, with Monday and Friday as remote work days. Exceptions require VP-level approval.

Remote work stipend: All employees receive a one-time $500 home office stipend upon hire to set up their remote workspace. See the Onboarding Guide for details on equipment stipend and how to submit your claim.

International remote work: Employees may work from outside their home country for up to 4 weeks per calendar year with manager approval. Tax implications are the employee's responsibility.

## 4. Expense Policy

Acme Corp reimburses reasonable business expenses according to the following approval tiers:

- **Under $200**: Auto-approved when submitted with receipt via Expensify
- **$200 - $1,000**: Requires direct manager approval
- **Over $1,000**: Requires VP-level approval

Common reimbursable expenses include: business travel, client meals, conference tickets, professional development courses, and work equipment. All expenses must be submitted within 30 days of purchase.

Travel policy: Domestic flights should be booked economy class. International flights over 6 hours may be booked premium economy. Hotels should not exceed $250/night in major metros or $175/night in other locations.

## 5. Benefits Overview

### Health Insurance
Acme Corp covers 90% of employee health insurance premiums and 70% of dependent premiums. Plans include medical, dental, and vision through Aetna. Open enrollment occurs annually in November.

### 401(k) Retirement Plan
Acme Corp matches employee contributions at 100% up to 4% of salary. The match follows a 4-year cliff vesting schedule. Employees are eligible to participate after 90 days of employment.

### Life Insurance
All employees receive company-paid life insurance equal to 2x annual salary, up to $500,000.

### Professional Development
Each employee receives a $1,500 annual learning budget for conferences, courses, books, and certifications. Unused budget does not roll over.

## 6. Performance Reviews

Performance reviews are conducted twice annually: mid-year (June) and end-of-year (December). Reviews use a 1-5 rating scale across four dimensions: Impact, Collaboration, Technical Excellence, and Leadership. Compensation adjustments are determined during the February compensation cycle based on end-of-year reviews.

Promotion cycles occur in March and September. Employees must have been in their current role for at least 12 months to be eligible.

## 7. Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to uphold Acme Corp's values: Integrity, Innovation, Inclusivity, and Impact. Harassment, discrimination, and retaliation of any kind are strictly prohibited. Violations should be reported to HR or through the anonymous ethics hotline at 1-800-ACME-ETH.

## 8. Separation and Offboarding

Voluntary resignation requires a minimum 2-week notice period (4 weeks for directors and above). Exit interviews are conducted by HR on the final day. All company equipment must be returned within 5 business days of the last working day. Final paychecks include any accrued but unused PTO.
